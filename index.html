I'm Bill Mayo, a New York-based developer and all-around fun guy. :) Originally from Maine, I moved to New York in 2004 and spent my days composing music for television and advertising. I started making the transition to the tech industry in 2015, and graduated from General Assembly's Web Developoment Immersive program in 2016. Now I build websites and web applications for individuals and businesses in New York and beyond.

My approach to web design is rooted in my music composition background. I value simplicity and structure, and always keep my eyes open to new influences.
