HEY KIDS!

I'm Bill Mayo, a New York-based developer and all-around fun guy. :) Originally from Maine, I moved to New York in 2004 and spent my days composing music for television and advertising. I started making the transition to the tech industry in 2015, and have been building web apps ever since. I've worked on mobile apps with React Native, deployed and maintained web apps with React.js, and contributed to front end code of all types.

My approach to web design is rooted in my music composition background. I value simplicity and structure, and always keep my eyes open to new influences.
